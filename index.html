SUMMARY LATITUDE vs X

The aim of this project is to showcase the changes that occur in the weather as you get closer to the equator. To get our results, we pulled data from OpenWeatherMap API, this included a dataset of over 500 cities. After analysing the dataset, we used Matplotlib to plot our 4 graph which showcased the relationship between latitude and different aspect of the weather, such as humidity, temperature, wind speed, and cloudiness.
